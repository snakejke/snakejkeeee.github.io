In the Dungeon Secret Realm, after entering the BOSS challenge, the team panel will generate the corresponding number of Jianxin Resonance Values according to the difficulty of the Secret Realm and the number of team members entering the battle.

  • Small Secret Realm: 5 people-7 people and 1 Jianxin, 8-10 people 2 Jianxin
  • Medium Secret Realm: 2 Kenshin for 10-15 people, 3 Kenshin for 16-20 people
  • The number of sword hearts will increase in the experience difficulty, please refer to the game content

The Jianxin Resonance Value is shared by the team, and the team members use Jianxin Resonance·Skills to consume the Jianxin Resonance Value.

Only Healing Specialization and Output Specialization can use "Sword Heart Resonance·Skills".

Healing specialization consumes 1 Jianxin and can resurrect a dead teammate.

The output specialization consumes 1 Jianxin, which can provide all members of the team with a BUFF that increases all attributes by 10% for 30 seconds, but the effect cannot be obtained again within 5 minutes.

 
In challenging instances, Jianxin is very important and needs to be used under the command of the team. Do not operate by yourself.
Sword skill

After the monster's golden body is broken, the sword skill will be triggered after using the sword skill pre skill on the monster, causing very high damage to the monster

After the sword skill is triggered, the pre skill CD becomes 1 minute, otherwise it is only 5 seconds.
The sword skill has a long period of rigidity. Do not use the sword skill when the boss is about to leave the position.
